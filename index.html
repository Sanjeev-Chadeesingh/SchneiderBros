Organic Family. Farming. Schneider Brothers

A family affair

The Schneider brothers have farming in their DNA. Three previous generations farmed in Nebraska, cultivating produce and cattle.

In 2019, they acquired Hidden Grove Farm in Florida. They decided a minimial footprint through responsible, organic horticulture would be their contribution to the Schneider farming legacy.

Organic

No pesticides. No chemicals. Ever. That's the promise, to you and us.

Organic farming requires the fostering of robust, healthy biodiversity. Our deep respect of the earth is evident the moment you set foot on the property.
